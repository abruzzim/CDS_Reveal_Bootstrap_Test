Essentials of DKA

  • Keep 'em dry and keep 'em sweet
  • Cerebral edema can develop even as labs get better!
  • Look for increasing corrected Na and gradual drop in Glu
  • OK

First Hour Overview

Place IV - 2 if possible

Draw Labs

Start IVF

NEUROCHECKS!

First Labs

  • Essential Labs:
    • Venous or Capillary Blood Gas with Electrolytes
    • Calculate Corrected Na
    • Blood Glu
  • BUN
  • Creat
  • CBC
  • Phosphorous

IV Fluids

  • Bolus with 20 mL/kg isotonic fluids only! NS, LR, Plasmalyte
  • Repeat 20 mL/kg ONLY if hypotensive
  • Goal of fluid resuscitation is to assure perfusion and restore blood pressure NOT to normalize HR and mentation
  • NO Bolus insulin
  • NO sodium bicarbonate UNLESS patient hypotensive or in cardiopulmonary arrest

If acute change in mentation...

  • 1st Choice: Push 6 mL/kg 3% NaCl (hypertonic saline) IV to raise Na by 5 mEq/dL
  • OR
  • 2nd Choice: Push 0.5 gm/kg mannitol IV
  • Consider CT scan
  • Consider controlling airway

Second Hour Overview

Recheck Blood Glu q 30 minutes

Assess labs sent in first hour

  • Check blood gas + electrolytes q 2 hr
    • Look for increase in corrected Na and pH (good), decr in K (expected)

Start insulin infusion

Start Maintenance IV Fluids

Neurochecks q 15 minutes!

Insulin Infusion

  • Use Regular Humulin Insulin
  • DO NOT use bolus insulin
  • 250 units/250mL NS at:
    • Infants to Toddlers: 0.05 units/kg/hr to 0.1 units/kg/hour
    • Everyone Else: 0.1 units/kg/hour
  • (Note: Maintenance IVF should already be running)

IV Fluids

All at 1.5 x Maintenance

  • If Glu > 250
    • If K ≥ 5.0 AND/OR NO urine output
      • NS
    • If K < 5.0 AND urine output documented
      • 1st choice: NS + 20 mEq KCl/L + 20 mEq Kphos/L
      • 2nd choice: NS + 40 mEq KCl/L
      • 3rd choice: NS + 20 mEq KCl/L
  • If Glu ≤ 250
    • If K < 5 AND urine output documented
      • 1st choice: D10 NS + 20 mEq KCl/L + 20 mEq Kphos/L
      • 2nd choice: D5 NS + 40 mEq KCl/L
      • 3rd choice: D5 NS + 20 mEq KCl/L
    • If K ≥ 5 and NO urine output
      • 1st choice: D10 NS
      • 2nd choice: D5 NS

If acute change in mentation...

  • 1st Choice: Push 6 mL/kg 3% saline (hypertonic saline) IV to raise blood sodium by 5 mEq/dL
  • OR
  • 2nd Choice: Push 0.5 gm/kg mannitol IV
  • Consider CT scan
  • Consider controlling airway

Post 2 Hour Overview

Check Neurochecks q 15 min

Check Glu Hourly

Check blood gas with electrolytes q 2 hourly

  • Look for increase in corrected Na and pH (good), decr in K (expected)

Ice chips generally ok to give patient for dry mouth but otherwise patient remains STRICTLY NPO

If acute change in mentation...

  • 1st Choice: Push 6 mL/kg 3% saline (hypertonic saline) IV to raise blood sodium by 5 mEq/dL
  • OR
  • 2nd Choice: Push 0.5 gm/kg mannitol IV
  • Consider CT scan
  • Consider controlling airway

Glucose

If Glu dropping by > 100 mg/dL/hr after initial fluid bolus AND already on dextrose-containing IVF, then

  • Increase rate to 2X maintenance (preferred) OR
  • TEMPORARILY decrease insulin infusion (from 0.1 units/kg/hr to 0.05 units/kg/hr)
